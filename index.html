This website serves as an online home for my projects and my online portfolio. It serves as a documentation of my journey to explore interests and passions. Check back now and again to see how (g)it's merged and branched off.

Research

  • Howard Y. Chang Laboratory (HHMI/Stanford University of Medicine)
  • RNA purification, Cell culture, Protein purification/crystallization/refinement
  • See list below.

I'm currently an undergraduate researcher in the Chang laboratory under the mentorship of Dr. Robert Spitale and Ryan Flynn. My current projects involve characterizing new proteininteractions that target non-coding RNA segments. Before joining the Chang lab, I performed structural studies to further rational vaccine design for RSV and HIV-1 at the Vaccine Research Center under the direction of Dr. Peter Kwong.

Outside of biomedical research, I have an independent research project that models organic chemistry using music theory that is under review. The manuscript for the model can be viewed here.
