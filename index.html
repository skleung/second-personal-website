Research

I'm fascinated by biological systems and the ongoing research to understand them. Last quarter, I was an undergraduate researcher in the Chang laboratory under the mentorship of Dr. Robert Spitale and Ryan Flynn. My current projects involve characterizing new protein interactions that target non-coding RNA segments.

Before joining the Chang lab, I performed structural studies to further rational vaccine design for RSV and HIV-1 at NIH's Vaccine Research Center under the direction of Dr. Jason Gorman and Dr. Jason McLellan.

Outside of biomedical research, I've been working on and off on an independent research project that models organic chemistry using music theory.
