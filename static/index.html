Research

I'm currently an undergraduate researcher in the Chang laboratory under the mentorship of Dr. Robert Spitale and Ryan Flynn. My current projects involve characterizing new proteininteractions that target non-coding RNA segments. Before joining the Chang lab, I performed structural studies to further rational vaccine design for RSV and HIV-1 at the Vaccine Research Center under the direction of Dr. Jason Gorman and Dr. Jason McLellan.

Outside of biomedical research, I have an independent research project that models organic chemistry using music theory that is under review.
